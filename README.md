### About Me

I'm a first-year M.S. Computer Science student at the University of Memphis (graduating May 2027), working as a Graduate Research Assistant in the AI & Cybersecurity Lab. Before grad school, I spent two years at Tata Consultancy Services as a Systems Engineer building backend services and data pipelines, plus an internship at Zemoso Technologies on full-stack development.

I enjoy working across the full stack — from data pipelines and backend services to frontend interfaces — and I'm especially drawn to projects where good engineering meets real research questions.
